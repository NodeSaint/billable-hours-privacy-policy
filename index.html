Billable Hours

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 March 2026

Billable Hours is developed by NUVIX. We take your privacy seriously — this extension is designed to work entirely on your device.

Data Storage

All data stays on your device. Your time entries, client list, and settings are stored locally using Chrome's Storage API. Nothing is transmitted, collected, or shared. There are no accounts, no servers, and no external connections.

Permissions Used

  • storage — save your time entries, client list, and settings locally
  • alarms — update the toolbar badge with your running timer

What This Extension Does Not Do

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Read or modify webpage content
  • Communicate with external servers
  • Use analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind
  • Sell or share data with third parties
  • Use data for advertising or profiling

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, changes will be posted here with a revised date.
